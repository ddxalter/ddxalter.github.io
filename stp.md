# Short-Term Plasticity

$$
\tau \frac{d P_\mathrm{rel}}{d t} = P_0 - P_\mathrm{rel}
$$

Fascilitation case,

$$
P_\mathrm{rel} \leftarrow P_\mathrm{rel} + f_F (1 - P_\mathrm{rel})
$$

Depression case,

$$
P_\mathrm{rel} \leftarrow f_D P_\mathrm{rel})
$$
